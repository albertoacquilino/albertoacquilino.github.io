Giovanni Louis - "1995" Album Recording Recording artist - Pop release (2022)

I contributed as a recording artist on "1995", composing and performing the solos for the single "Carmen" and tracking parts alongside the production team. The record blends contemporary pop production with storytelling drawn from Giovanni Louis's experience growing up in Genoa, Italy.

About the artist

Giovanni Louis (Genoa, 1995) is an emerging Italian pop artist. He began his musical studies very young at the Niccolò Paganini Conservatory, has performed over the years as a musician and singer at international festivals, and records for film and television. He graduated as a pop performer, composer, and songwriter at CET di Mogol, was a finalist at the Universal award "Genova per voi" and was invited to the Club Tenco for his "ability to combine highly literate lyrics with shamelessly pop arrangements." The album "1995" was released on 03/28/22 and has totaled hundreds of thousands of play streams.
